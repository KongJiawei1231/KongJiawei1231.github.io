Biography

I am a first-year master's student in the ITML lab in the SIGS, Tsinghua University, where I am fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Shu-Tao Xia. I also work closely with Prof. Bin-Chen. I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, in 2025.

My research interests primarily include Trustworthy AI and Generative Models, with a particular focus on the reliability and robustness of Large Reasoning Models.
